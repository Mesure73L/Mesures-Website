When do I stream?

I stream every once in a while. I try to stream every week, but realistically about once a month. When I do stream, it's most likely around 2:30 am UTC (not my timezone).

Why can't viewers chat when I'm not live?

Due to moderation purposes, only Twitch subscribers (doesn't exist yet) can chat. This is to make sure that nothing bad happens when I'm not watching my chat.

How do I make automations?

I use Streamer.bot to automate my livestreams. It is not the best for non-technical users to explore but easy to follow along with tutorials.
