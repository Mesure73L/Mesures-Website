Thingy Bingy Translator

What is Thingy Bingy?

Thingy Bingy is a language created by Mesure73L and RobotSlayer. The translator was originally created by RobotSlayer, and modified by Mesure73L to make Thingy Bingy as good as it can possibly be. Every word with more than 2 characters will have "ing" added after the second character, and all words will have "ingy" put at the end.
