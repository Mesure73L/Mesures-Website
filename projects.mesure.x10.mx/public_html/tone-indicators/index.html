Tone Indicators

How To Use Tone Indicators 🔗

Do not use tone indicators as a joke. Tone indicators can be very helpful, especially for neurodivergent people. Don't be a dick, and don't misuse tone indicators.

Do not make fun of tone indicators. Some people need a little extra help understanding the meaning of what you are saying. Tone indicators can be extremely useful.

Try not to use more than 2 tone indicators. The more tone indicators, the more confusing your message is. Do not say, "You're a good friend. /srs /gen /nx /pos /li," instead say, "You're a good friend. /gen /pos."

Try to include tone indicators in the original post. Some people forget to add a tone indicator, and later reply to it with a tone indicator after a lot of people have seen it.

Not everyone wants tone indicators. Some people don't need the extra help and think that they are a distraction. If you aren't sure, just ask. (If they don't know what you're talking about, link them to this page!)

Tone indicators can be before or after a message. Sometimes, if tone is really important, you should put the tone indicator before the message so people read it first. Some examples are "/cw" (content warning) or "/tw" (trigger warning).

You can use multple different tones in one message. If different messages are different tones, you can use different tones for each sentance. Just don't put them after every sentance.

"/j" ruins the joke. If you are telling a joke, you don't need to use any tone indicators. If someone tells you something's a joke afterwards, it ruins the whole joke.

Use quotation marks when mentioning a tone indicator. If you are mentioning a tone indicator, either write out the meaning or put it in quotation marks. For example, write, "My favorite tone indicator is the '/ij' tone indicator," or "My favorite tone indicator is the inside joke tone indicator," instead of "My favorite tone indicator is /ij."
